# Beta Feedback — AI Support Assistant
Collected from first 18 beta users, Nairobi + Lagos

## Positive
- "Responses come back fast, way faster than waiting for a support agent."
- Several users liked that it could handle account questions without
  escalation — 12 of 18 said their first question was fully resolved.
- Users appreciated it working over a slow connection — text-only kept it
  usable even on 3G.

## Negative
- 7 of 18 users tried asking questions in Swahili and got English-only
  responses back, or responses that didn't understand the question at all.
- Two users reported the assistant repeating the same suggestion twice in
  a longer conversation.
- One user in Lagos flagged that it didn't recognize a local payment method
  (bank transfer via a specific local app) and gave generic advice instead.

## Feature requests
- Swahili and Pidgin language support (came up unprompted from 6 users).
- Ability to escalate to a human without restarting the whole conversation.
- WhatsApp integration — most requested channel, ahead of email or web chat.

## Summary
Overall sentiment is cautiously positive — users like the speed and the
offline-friendly design, but language support is the clear blocker before
a wider rollout. Kevin's localization work (see meeting notes) directly
addresses the top complaint.
